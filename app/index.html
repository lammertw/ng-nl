February 13, 2015 - Amsterdam

NG-NL

The NG-NL Conference is the first AngularJS conference in The Netherlands, organized by Xebia and the Dutch AngularJS Group. It took place at Pakhuis De Zwijger in Amsterdam om February 13, 2015. Please follow us on Twitter at @ngnlconf to stay up to date.

See you again in 2016

NG-NL 2015 was a great success. We're already looking forward to 2016. Expect us back around the same time next year. Meanwhile, keep an eye on our Youtube Channel for recordings of the talks on the main track. Thanks for joining us!

If you want to stay informed about important NG-NL announcements, next year's edition and early bird promotions, leave your info in the form below.

Speakers

Maurice de Beijer

Maurice de Beijer is an independent software consultant and trainer specializing in HTML5, REST, JavaScript, AngularJS and ASP.NET MVC. For DevelopMentor he currently teaches Essential and Advanced AngularJS, Essential HTML5, Essential jQuery, Modern Web Development and Essential ASP.NET MVC courses. He has been awarded the yearly Microsoft Most Valuable Professional award since 2005. Maurice is also very active in the Dutch dotNed user group where he regularly organizes meetings and the dotNed-Pluralsight study group meetings.

Roy Brondgeest

I guess I am a good example of a polyglot programmer. Graduated in Embedded Systems, and professionally worked with quite a few languages developing desktop, mobile and web apps over 2 decades. I am currently involved in architecting and building enterprise applications with Java EE, Scala and AngularJS. I am really intrigued about Scala and the reactive manifesto, and I am eager to share my enthusiasm with you!

Christoph Burgdorf

Christoph began with programming at the age of 10 with BASIC but has since moved on to become proficient in various different programming languages and technologies. He has contributed to many projects, including AngularJS, jquery-ui, TodoMVC and the banshee media player and is also the creator of the nickel.rs framework. When he's not evangelizing Git, he likes to travel the world with his bicycle.

Olivier Combe

Self-learning web developer, Olivier Combe loves everything front-end related and especially Javascript & CSS. His latest crush has been Angular for the last 2 years. He firmly believes in open-source and has been contributing to many projects on his free time.

Ofir Dagan

I'm Ofir, a senior software engineer at WIX.com. In the last couple of years I've become a true “TDD believer” and a mentor at heart. I recently mentored in the last GDG (Google Developers Group) Hackathon in Israel and at WIX academy (WIX's training program). Besides that I'm an adrenaline enthusiastic. From skydiving to bungee through surfing, diving, snowboarding and climbing. If it has adrenaline I've done it.

Kent C. Dodds

I am Kent C. Dodds. I work at Alianza as the frontend developer working with AngularJS primarily. I co-host Angular Air, a video podcast about AngularJS, with my friend and GDE Todd Motto. I'm an Egghead.io instructor. I'm happily married and happily the father of two kids. I like code. I care about craft, design, and architecture. I like to talk about it. Come chat with me :-)

Aaron Frost

Aaron is part of Domo's crackshot Frontend Dev team. He was nominated by Google to be a Google Developer Expert for AngularJS. He is a panelist on JavaScript Jabber and Adventures in Angular. Aaron is also working on an ES6 book with O'Reilly Media. He is an accomplished speaker, having presented all around the world. He is also one of the organizers of ng-conf, the Angular community's largest developer conference of the year. His greatest accomplishment of all, however, is his beautiful family, including his wife and four children.

Gert Hengeveld

Gert is a Full Stack Developer at Xebia working with Scala, Akka and AngularJS. He's the author of several popular articles on AngularJS and regularly speaks at meetups. Angular has been his weapon of choice for the past 2.5 years, with at least half a dozen apps running in production.

Raúl Jiménez

My expertise covers AngularJS, JavaScript, HTML5/CSS3, ActionScript and Media Streaming. I'm passionate about cross-platform applications, devices and screens and I'm trying to make the web better with my project Videogular.

Kees de Kooter

Kees de Kooter is a seasoned independent full-stack developer 'with an itch'. He always pursues the creation of software solutions that are simple, easily maintainable and highly extensible. He is on a continuous quest for 'the right tool for the right job’. He currently lives in WebStorm and his favourite tool is Rotterdam.

Pawel Kozlowski

Pawel is software-development addict that believes in free, open source software. He is a core contributor to AngularJS framework, AngularUI, Karma-runner and several other projects. He is the co-author the "Mastering Web Application Development with AngularJS" book. When not coding, Pawel can be spotted speaking at various software development conferences.

Daniel Marjenburgh

I am a Full Stack Developer and Consultant at Xebia who is passionate about building elegant software and delivering great quality. I try to stay on top of things, keep learning new ways of doing and ponder about ways we can make our software better and simpler.

He loves contributing to open source and is the creator of the popular angular-translate module. Pascal loves to travel the world and speaks at conferences and meetups. Haiko is an experienced full stack software engineer currently working at the Dutch Railways. He is passionate about open source software and clean, simple coding. With a Java background, he recently entered the AngularJS world feeling like a kid in the candy store. Dave Smith

Dave has been developing software professionally for 10 years. He got his start tinkering on a TI-82 calculator in high school and has worked on projects that span the spectrum from embedded devices to user interfaces. Currently, he works at HireVue on the company’s Angular-based web application.

Manfred Steyer (www.softwarearchitekt.at) is a freelance trainer, consultant and author at IT-Visions (www.it-visions.at) and also responsible for the specialist division "Software-Engineering" at the university of applied sciences CAMPUS 02 (www.campus02.at) in Graz (Austria).
