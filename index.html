February 13, 2015 - Amsterdam

NG-NL

The NG-NL Conference in Amsterdam is the first AngularJS Conference that will take place in The Netherlands. One full day we will be hosting sessions and workshops about AngularJS and related topics.

All sessions will be in English and the conference will be from 9am to 5pm (doors will open earlier for registration). All Early Bird tickets are sold out! Regular tickets are available now. Please follow us on twitter at @ngnlconf to stay up to date.

Featured Speakers

More speakers will be announced soon...

Pawel Kozlowski

Pawel is software-development addict that believes in free, open source software. He is a core contributor to AngularJS framework, AngularUI, Karma-runner and several other projects. He is the co-author the "Mastering Web Application Development with AngularJS" book. When not coding, Pawel can be spotted speaking at various software development conferences.

Aaron Frost

Aaron is part of Domo's crackshot Frontend Dev team. He was nominated by Google to be a Google Developer Expert for AngularJS. He is a panelist on JavaScript Jabber and Adventures in Angular. Aaron is also working on an ES6 book with O'Reilly Media. He is an accomplished speaker, having presented all around the world. He is also one of the organizers of ng-conf, the Angular community's largest developer conference of the year. His greatest accomplishment of all, however, is his beautiful family, including his wife and four children.
